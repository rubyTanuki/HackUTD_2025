Our Mission

Scholar Scraper is an application that was created in order to assist college students in their research process. It gives users the ability to input a research thesis and review a list of ranked sources based off of matching keywords.

Who We Are

Our mission is to empower learners and professionals by connecting their work to the right knowledge, driving innovation and discovery.

We prioritize user experience and accessibility, making academic research faster and easier for everyone.

Collaboration and innovation are at the core of our approach, ensuring our tools and resources evolve with your needs.
